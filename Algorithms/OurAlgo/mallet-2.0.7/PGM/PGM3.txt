Since 1877, Pacific Garden Mission has been a place of refuge and transformation. Millions weary of struggling through life's storms, have come to the "Old Lighthouse seeking food, shelter, clothing, medical, and dental care.
They also came seeking the answer to life's struggles. At Pacific Garden Mission they are offered the answer: life through faith in Jesus Christ. But the power of the Holy Spirit doesnt stop with salvation. PGM has a mission to provide discipleship to men and women who profess faith in Jesus.
